HAHSY Industries, Inc.

Leader in Quality Food Packaging and Cleaning Products

Founded in 2011, Hahsy Industries, Inc. produces and distributes paper made products such as paper cups, bowls, plates, boxes, trays, and bags all throughout the country.

The company is focused on manufacturing eco-friendly alternative food packaging products of different types and sizes for the catering industry. Hahsy Industries, Inc. is in rapid growth mode, with huge opportunities for advancement and prosperity.
